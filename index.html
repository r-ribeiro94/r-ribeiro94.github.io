About Me

Hello, my name is Rogerio and I am a full stack developer that is passionate about web development.

As of Fall 2016, I am a graduate of Ryerson University with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science. During my time at Ryerson, I became very interested in many web related topics either as a front-end or back-end contributor.


Starting out only as an assignment during my 4th year, I helped develop a scheduling application along with two other colleagues of the Computer Science program. My colleagues and I were encouraged to continue working on this application from our professor as we have currently taken time out of our schedules to do so. The tasks were distributed equally as my contribution to this project was related to the front-end development of the application. The project was later completed by the winter of 2016.


Outside of academics, my interests lie in playing video games and participating in sports such as soccer. Through these activities I have become a more team focused, result oriented and reliable person. These traits have allowed me to solve everyday problems and thrive in a team environment with an improved focus in achieving goals and going beyond expectations.
